OhWomaniya

The problem

Networking is crucial in today's era. But many women either hesitate to network or some conditions prevent them from networking. Also many women face challenges while setting up business such as limited funding, limited knowledge, personality issues and many more. The major problem being the funding.

The solution

A social media platform where women can connect, get mentored or give guidance to women from various spheres of life, get financial aid for your dream business and help similar ones who are building theirs and learn from resources such as articles, videos or participate in workshops and online events to develop their career.

User goals

  • To get financial aid.
  • To get a connection with women in a similar field of interest.
  • To share their concerns and fears without being judged.
  • To get guidance to have a work-life balance.
  • To get knowledge about their field .

Business Goals

  • To sign up more women.
  • To add more investors.
  • To generate more revenue through participation.
  • To provide a safe space for women to network with one another.
